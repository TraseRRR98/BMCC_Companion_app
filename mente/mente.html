About Mente+

Mente+ is here to provide compassionate, confidential, and convenient mental health support anytime you need it.

Our mission is to make mental health support accessible, personalized, and stigma-free for all students.

Features

  • Real-Time Emotional Support: Mente+ listens and responds to you with empathy.
  • Guided Exercises: Access mindfulness practices, stress management techniques, and more.
  • Resource Recommendations: Connects you to BMCC counseling services and wellness resources.
  • Mood Tracking: Monitor your emotions over time and gain insights into your mental well-being.
